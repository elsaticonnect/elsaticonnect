How It Works

Structured flows for businesses, suppliers, and the procurement decisions between them.

Elsati Connect gives each side of the trade journey a clearer experience, then connects both through verified suppliers, digital quotation tools, and stronger procurement visibility.

Business how it works

How businesses move from registration to better procurement decisions.

Businesses register, define their requirements, invite verified suppliers, and compare quotations in a cleaner digital process.

01

Register your business

Create your profile and establish the procurement team that will manage sourcing on the platform.

02

Create an RFQ

Publish request details, documents, timelines, and evaluation expectations in one place.

03

Invite verified suppliers

Reach suppliers that have already passed verification standards accepted across buyers.

04

Compare and award

Use side-by-side quotation views and reports to select the best fit with less leakage and more control.

Supplier how it works

How suppliers register, respond, and stay visible to buyers.

Suppliers join the network, complete verification, respond to client RFQs digitally, and track progress without chasing updates manually.

01

Register as supplier

Build your supplier profile and prepare your business for visibility across buyer networks.

02

Complete verification

Submit the details required for validation through physical site visits and business checks.

03

Receive and respond to RFQs

Open buyer requests, review requirements, and submit quotations digitally in a standard format.

04

Track outcomes

Monitor quote status, buyer review progress, and award decisions in one supplier dashboard.
