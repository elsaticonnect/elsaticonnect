About Elsati Connect

Building trusted digital infrastructure for modern procurement.

Elsati Connect is designed to help businesses source verified suppliers, manage procurement requests, compare quotations, and make confident sourcing decisions through one structured digital platform.

View Workflow Contact Us
Mission

To simplify procurement by making supplier discovery, quotation management, and sourcing decisions more transparent and efficient.

The Problem

Procurement is still too manual, fragmented, and difficult to trust.

Many businesses still rely on WhatsApp, emails, calls, and spreadsheets to identify suppliers, request quotations, compare prices, and make procurement decisions. This slows teams down and makes supplier performance difficult to track.

Our Solution

A structured workspace for buyers, suppliers, and sourcing teams.

Elsati Connect brings supplier discovery, RFQs, quotation comparison, supplier records, and procurement visibility into one digital environment built around trust, speed, and accountability.
